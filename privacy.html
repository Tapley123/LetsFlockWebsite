Privacy Policy — Let’s Flock!

Effective Date: January 1, 2025
Developer: Tapley Games

Important Note

Our applications, including Let’s Flock!, are intended for users aged 13 years or older. We do not knowingly collect or store personal information from children under 13 years of age, in compliance with the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). If we learn that we have collected data from a child under 13 without verified parental consent, we will delete that information promptly.

Information We Collect

We collect only the data required to operate core gameplay features such as leaderboards and achievements:

  • Meta/Oculus UUID: Used to identify players for leaderboard ranking, score comparison, and entitlement verification.
  • Meta/Oculus Username: Displayed in-game for leaderboards and social features.
  • Score Data: Includes high scores and related gameplay metrics used for comparison.
  • Leaderboard API Data: UUIDs, usernames, and associated statistics retrieved via Meta’s Leaderboard API and Microsoft PlayFab services.
  • Achievements API Data: Used to display completed or unlocked achievements in-game.

No personally identifiable information such as real names, email addresses, or contact details is collected directly by Let’s Flock!.

How We Use This Information

  • To display in-game leaderboards and achievements.
  • To verify entitlement and maintain fair gameplay.
  • To improve player experience and general performance analytics.

Collected data is used only within the context of the game. We do not sell, rent, or share user data with third parties for marketing purposes.

Data Processors and Service Providers

To provide online features, we use trusted service providers:

  • Meta Platforms, Inc.: Provides the Oculus Platform and stores user profile data.
  • Microsoft Corporation (PlayFab): Hosts leaderboard and achievement data on Microsoft Azure servers.

These providers process limited user information on our behalf under strict confidentiality and security standards.

International Data Transfers

Data may be processed in the United States, Ireland, Netherlands, and Singapore where these services maintain servers. By using the Game, you consent to such transfers for the purpose of providing platform features.

Data Security

We use reasonable administrative and technical measures to help protect information. All leaderboard and achievement data are handled through secure platform APIs and stored within the service environments of Meta and PlayFab.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, or request deletion of your data processed through the Game. To exercise these rights, contact us at the email below, and we will coordinate with Meta or Microsoft as needed.

Children’s Privacy

Let’s Flock! is not directed to children under 13. Parents or guardians who believe a child has provided personal data without consent may contact us, and we will remove the data as required by law.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any material changes will be reflected in the latest version available within the Meta Quest Store listing and on our official website.
