Privacy Policy — Let’s Flock!

Effective Date: January 1, 2025
Developer: Tapley Games

Important Note

Our applications, including Let’s Flock!, are intended for users aged 13 years or older. We do not knowingly collect or store personal information from children under 13 years of age, in compliance with the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). If we learn that we have collected data from a child under 13 without verified parental consent, we will delete that information promptly.

Information We Collect

We collect only the data required to operate core gameplay features such as leaderboards and achievements:

  • Meta/Oculus UUID: Used to identify players for leaderboard ranking and score comparison.
  • Meta/Oculus Username: Displayed in-game for leaderboards and social features.
  • Score Data: Includes high scores and related gameplay metrics used for comparison.
  • Leaderboard API Data: UUIDs, usernames, and associated statistics retrieved via Meta’s Leaderboard API.
  • Achievements API Data: Used to display completed or unlocked achievements in-game.

No personally identifiable information such as real names, email addresses, or contact details is collected directly by Let’s Flock!.

How We Use This Information

  • To display in-game leaderboards and achievements.
  • To maintain fair gameplay and accurate player ranking.
  • To improve player experience and general performance analytics.

Collected data is used only within the context of the game. We do not sell, rent, or share user data with third parties.

Data Security

We use reasonable administrative and technical measures to help protect information. All leaderboard and achievement data are handled through secure Meta platform APIs and stored within Meta’s service environment when applicable.

Children’s Privacy

Let’s Flock! is not directed to children under 13. Parents or guardians who believe a child has provided personal data without consent may contact us, and we will remove the data as required by law.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any material changes will be reflected in the latest version available within the Meta Quest Store listing and on our official website.
